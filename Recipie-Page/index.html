Mushroom Curry

This was a reicipe I picked up after I returned home from the field, and was stuffed full of rich, calorie-laden camp food. This was something much more wholesome than the stuff I was eating on a regular basis.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 roughly-chopped onion
  • 1 large potato, chopped into large chunks
  • 1 aubergine, chopped into chunks
  • 250g of mushrooms
  • 2-4 tbsp of curry paste
  • 150ml Vegetable stock
  • 1 can of coconut milk (400ml)
  • Chopped Coriander

Directions

  • Prep: 30min
  • Cook: 40min
  • Total: 1hr, 10min
  1. In a saucepan, heat the oil then add the onion and potato. Cover the pan, then cook over light heat until the potatoes start to soften.
  2. Add the eggplant and button mushroms then cook for a few more minutes
  3. Stir in the curry paste, and add the stock and the coconut milk.
  4. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 10 minutes or until the potato is tender.
  5. Stir throuh the coriander, and serve alongside rice or with naan bread.
